DPI

versus

PPI

DPI

Dots per inch

PPI

Pixels per inch

DPI

This is the amount of ink dots the printer will put on each pixel of your image.

PPI

Digital raster images are measured in pixels, or picture elements.

DPI

The DPI is set by the actual printer device and it is not something in the image for the graphic designer to manipulate.

PPI

How many pixels per inch is determined by the device you create the digital image with: camera, scanner, or graphics software and can be modified with a photo/paint editing software.

Father of Pointillism

Georges Seurat
(2 December 1859 - 29 March 1891)
Seurat was born in Paris in 1859, as the child of a religious family with a good economic situation.
Georges Seurat, one of the founders of the new impressionism, which based the theory of painting on the division of colors and optical mixing, managed to create extraordinary works in seven years. Seuret, who had a great interest in institutional and plastic research, studied the laws of simultaneous contrast of colors discovered by Chevreul and the theories of Eugène Delacroix, starting from 1876.
